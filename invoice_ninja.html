What is Invoice Ninja?

Technology company

Launched at the beginning of 2014 with the purpouse of creating applications for people and small business: Lanzada en inicios del 2014 con el fin de crear aplicaciones para personas y pequeños negocios: Billing, quotes, proposals, time tracking, project management.

Nearly limitless free program to our SaaS User-base.

Being open source, they offer users the option to self-host the Invoice Ninja platform on their own servers.

As a small business company

They build and refine their users' tools week after week. They offer the tools to facilitate business and billing

Boot-strapped & Open Source.

4 years and 60,000 users

After 4 years and more than 60,000 users, invoice ninja is consolidated as a billing company that has the ability to compete with the largest companies in the industry.

Open Source

The open source nature of the application offers a level of testing and quality which is difficult to match. When the latest updates are deployed, they have already been tested by thousands of developers around the world and have been deployed on hundreds of servers.

The result?

A billing tool that contains all the tools you may need, totally free.

Constant evolution.

Guided by the users

New features and functionalities are built and developed every month. These have been requested by users. It is possible to communicate with the creators and make a requesst for a new feature, which may be added in a future iteration..










Direct communication
